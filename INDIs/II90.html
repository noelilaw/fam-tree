Personal and Family Information

Joselito was the son of Cipriano "Totong" Francisco and Modesta "Moding" Reyes Francisco. The date and place of his birth have not been found.

His wife was Alice Franscisco. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their three known children were Melanie Conway Francisco (?-?), Joseph Michel Conway (?-?) and Joselito "Joey" Conway (?-?).
